PalDrop allows you to add a simple and fast payment button for your Dropbox files!
It combines your Paypal email with your Dropbox account and enables you to sell
files out of your Dropbox folders within seconds.

This Wordpress plugin is totally free and comes with an account for up to
10 products. Additional products require a subscription to paldrop.com.

== Description ==

The fastest way to sell files hosted on Dropbox! With this plugin you will get:

* a free Paldrop merchant account (for up to 10 registered products)
* an integrated button in your admin panel to fire up the member area
* a sidebar widget, that links with your ID to paldrop and lists your products

Paldrop.com is the standard way to sell files hosted on Dropbox and gives you 
access to **millions of legal digital goods** that you may additionally add to your
blog.

Read more about the [Paldrop features](http://www.paldrop.com/ "Downloads")
here. The current version provides English accounts and will be enhanced to
other languages down the road.

== Installation ==

The installation of the Paldrop Shop is pretty straight forward:

1. Upload the `paldrop` directory to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Create your free account by choosing a username
4. Enter the member area and start registering products
